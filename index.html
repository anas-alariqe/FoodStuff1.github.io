Adham Al-Hutheily (AH) FoodStuff Beginings:

Adham Al-Hutheily (AH) FoodStuff, was founded in the Republic of Yemen in the City of Sana'a at the beginning of the year of 2020. "AH" Company administrative team has a long experience in the field of local trade for about 23 years in several commercial fields, such as; FoodStuff, Poultry Farms and Transports.

Our Values:


- That "AH" Company name remains respected in the market by our customers, clients, as well as by our employees.

- That our dealings are based on honesty and transparency at every step.

- That our relationship with external and internal companies is strong.

Our Mission:



To keep "AH" Company name number one in providing the best foodstuff in the Republic of Yemen, by ensuring the quality of the product at an acceptable price, which helps the economy to stand and benefit.

Our Goal:



To move forward strongly, seeking the expansion of our company and focusing on creating job opportunities for the people of our country. Planning that by adding more branches and centers for "AH" Company in Yemen.
